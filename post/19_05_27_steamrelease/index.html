Steam Workshop Release

Monday May 27, 2019 by Swept

 

Patch v.0.4.6 | Protocol 15

Rimworld Multiplayer has finally been released on the Steam Workshop! https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1752864297

We decided to do this a bit earlier then planned because of several reasons

  • A lot of people were having trouble installing the mod

  • Updating the mod will be really easy for everyone

  • Using outdated versions of the mod won’t be a problem anymore

  • The next update of rimworld will break the mod for anyone who doesn’t update

We are also working on 2 custom-made Discord bots, in order to provide you with a better experience here. To ensure you’ll be happy with the publicly used bot, we would like to ask you to submit features you want to see implemented into the bot which can be done here: https://forms.gle/ifcU3AcHXNpXzuUd6
